Tuning Lab

Pick a target tuning — the needle guides every string toward it.

New strings drift — stretch them gently and retune a few times. Temperature changes and capos knock tuning out too; a 30-second check before practice is normal.

Capo Check

Clamp the capo just behind the fret, square across the neck. Then pick each string once, thickest first.

Fret-12 Intonation Test

The 12th fret should sound exactly one octave above the open string. Pick a string, then follow the two steps. Fret lightly, fingertip just behind the fret.

A string that reads sharp at fret 12 no matter what is an instrument setup issue (saddle position) — a guitar tech's five-minute fix, not your fingers' fault.

Note Hunt

20 questions. Find the named note on the named string — the app hears your answer. Uses your current tuning.

About Campfire

A guitar practice app that listens — built from classical signal processing, not AI. Everything runs on this phone: no accounts, no servers, no tracking, no network requests at all. Your audio is analysed live and never recorded or sent anywhere.
